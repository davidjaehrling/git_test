Proteinmuffins

These protein-packed muffins are perfect for a quick breakfast or snack, providing a healthy dose of protein and energy to fuel your day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Oats
  • 1 scoop Protein powder (flavor of choice)
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 ripe Bananas
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon Baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon Cinnamon (optional)
  • 1/4 cup Honey or Maple Syrup (optional)
  • 1/2 cup Mixed berries (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, mash the bananas until smooth.
  3. Add the eggs, Greek yogurt, and honey or maple syrup (if using) to the mashed bananas and mix well.
  4. In another bowl, combine the oats, protein powder, baking powder, and cinnamon.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until just combined.
  6. If using, fold in the mixed berries gently.
  7. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups.
  8. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Let cool before serving.
